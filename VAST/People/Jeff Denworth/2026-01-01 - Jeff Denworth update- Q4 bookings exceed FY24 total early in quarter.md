# Jeff Denworth update: Q4 bookings exceed FY24 total early in quarter

**Date**: 2026-01-01
**With**: Jeff Denworth, Myself

## Summary

Jeff Denworth shared a company-wide update that VAST Data recorded $256M in gross bookings in all of FY24 and has already reached $300M in gross bookings in the first two months of the current Q4. He noted that historically most Q4 bookings close in the final month, implying potential for a strong year-end finish.

- Jeff Denworth stated that VAST Data captured $256M of gross bookings in all of FY24, which he said established VAST as a $9B company.

- Jeff Denworth stated that VAST Data has $300M of gross bookings on the books in the first two months of the current Q4, exceeding the full FY24 gross bookings total.

- Jeff Denworth stated that VAST Data typically closes the bulk of quarterly bookings in the third and final month of the quarter.

- Jeff Denworth stated that 80% of VAST Data Q4 bookings landed in the final month of Q4 last year.
