## Summary

Jeff Denworth shared a Q4 bookings update stating VAST Data has $300M in gross bookings in the first two months of the quarter, exceeding the $256M captured in all of FY24. He noted that historically most Q4 bookings close in the final month, implying potential for a strong year-end finish.

- Jeff Denworth stated that VAST Data typically closes the bulk of the quarter's bookings in the third and final month.
