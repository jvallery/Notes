## Decisions


- Reframe Model Builder Turbine outreach to lead with concrete AI pipeline components (for example, scalable Kafka for reinforcement learning and a real-time vector database) rather than generic platform narratives to improve resonance with model builder technical buyers.

- Use an AI Circle event with NVIDIA customers as the primary mechanism to facilitate NVIDIA-related introductions to model builders, because direct 1:1 intros from NVIDIA representatives are unlikely.

- Broaden persona targeting for model builder outreach to include data and pipeline owners in addition to CTO and executive contacts to increase entry points and bottom-up sponsorship.

- Defer or avoid introduction paths that create hyperscaler conflicts (for example, Google or Microsoft conflicts) when those conflicts could undermine willingness to introduce or participate.
