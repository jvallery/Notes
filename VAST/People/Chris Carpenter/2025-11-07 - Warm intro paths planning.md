## Decisions
- Reframe outreach to focus on concrete pipeline components (e.g., scalable Kafka for RL, real-time vector DB) rather than generic platform narratives.
- Use an AI Circle NVIDIA-customer event as the primary mechanism for NVIDIA-facilitated introductions; 1:1 intros from NVIDIA reps are unlikely.
- Broaden persona targeting to include data/pipeline owners in addition to CTO/exec-level contacts.
- Defer direct Google/Microsoft-introduced paths where hyperscaler conflicts exist to reduce risk and friction.
