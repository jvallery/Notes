## Key Facts

- Leidos is pursuing Dept of Defense and Civilian Hybrid Cloud Strategies and positioning VAST Data as a foundational component of their 'Mission OS'.

- Leidos Federal is pursuing Dept of Defense and Civilian Hybrid Cloud Strategies.

- Leidos leaders are expected to speak at VAST Forward about how Leidos is using VAST Data in production environments.

- Leidos Federal opportunity FOpp-4197 (Opp Name: "Leidos DoD Cloud") was submitted to VAST Order Admin with Opp Amount/TCV of $96,808.14 and Close Date 2025-12-26.

- The Leidos Federal deal FOpp-4197 is a cloud deployment in a Leidos GovCloud instance and is software-only (no hardware).

- The win rationale recorded in the Order Admin alert emphasized hybrid cloud strategies for defense and civilian public sectors and cited VAST capabilities including ABAC, Global Namespace, Similarity, DASE (on-prem), and site-to-site meshed replication technology.

- Opportunity FOpp-4197 ("Leidos DoD Cloud") for Leidos Federal was submitted to VAST Order Admin with TCV $96,808.14 and close date 2025-12-26.

- The FOpp-4197 deal is a software-only cloud deployment in a Leidos GovCloud instance and was described as the first Cloud PO in the US Federal sector.
