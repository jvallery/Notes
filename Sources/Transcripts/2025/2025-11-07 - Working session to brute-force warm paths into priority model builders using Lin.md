## Outcomes (progress this session)

- Agreed to engage data/pipeline teams alongside senior execs for bottom-up and top-down coverage.

## Decisions (architecture, resourcing, priority)
- Reframe outreach to focus on concrete pipeline components (Kafka for RL, real-time vector DB) rather than platform narratives; improves resonance and response likelihood.
- Pursue NVIDIA-customer event (via AI Circle) as the primary NVIDIA intro mechanism; 1:1 direct intros from NVIDIA reps are unlikely.
- Broaden persona targeting to include data/pipeline owners, not just CTO-level contacts; increases entry points and reverses if exec paths re-open.
- Defer direct Google/Microsoft-introduced paths where conflicts exist; reduces risk of hyperscaler conflicts undermining intros.

## Risks (schedule, technical, external deps)
- Warm-intro threads cooling or stalling (e.g., Zara thread), reducing momentum.
- Reliance on weak LinkedIn mutuals yields low-quality intros and wasted cycles.
- Hyperscaler conflicts (Microsoft/Google) limit willingness to introduce or participate.
- Some targets may be locked into incumbent clouds or vendors (Oracle/Google), limiting immediate opportunity.
- Over-indexing on execs risks missing functional owners who can sponsor bottom-up pilots.
